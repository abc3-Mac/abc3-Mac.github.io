CastPolish v1.7.1

A free, local alternative to Auphonic. Loudness normalization, AI noise reduction, Whisper transcription, AI shownotes, and speaker diarization — all on your machine.

✅ macOS ✅ Linux ✅ Windows
🎯 Quality presets + vocabulary hints
🎚️ EBU R128 loudness (−16 LUFS)
🔇 AI noise reduction (DeepFilterNet3)
📝 Whisper transcription + captions
📄 One-click PDF & Word document export
📑 Embedded MP3/M4A chapter markers
⚠️ Confidence review in the editor
⏹ Cancellable processing jobs

Lutheran Lectionary

✝ LCMS / LSB ✅ Mac / Linux / Docker
✠ Historic Lent: readings + introit & collect for every weekday
📖 LSB Daily Lectionary — every day
🎵 Hymn of the Day (both series)
✝ One-Year propers: introit, collect & gradual
🌙 Almanac: moon, Easter, Passover & date tools
🇺🇸 U.S. civil holidays beside the church day
📅 iCal feeds + card & plain PDF sheets
📜 ESV, KJV & ASV scripture popups
⚙️ Settings: violet Advent, dark mode, search

ESSFTA Quarterly Rankings

Fourteen years of English Springer Spaniel Field Trial Association rankings — Breed/Group, Obedience, Agility & Rally — gathered from dozens of separate PDFs into one searchable page.

🐕 ESSFTA Parent Club ✅ Web · Ghost & WordPress-ready
🐾 All four sports in one place, 2013–2026
🔎 Search, filter & sort by dog or owner
🏆 ~2,800 rankings · ~1,900 dogs
📄 One-click PDF export of any view
ℹ️ Built-in title/class history per sport
🔤 Names cleaned, AKC titles preserved
⚖️ Ties preserved · honest coverage notes
🗄️ Every figure traces to an official report
🏅 Working Dog (WD/WDX) title awards, 2012–2025
