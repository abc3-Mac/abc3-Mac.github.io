Featured Project

CastPolish

A free, local, open-source alternative to Auphonic for podcast audio post-production. Loudness normalization, noise reduction, Whisper transcription, AI shownotes, and speaker diarization — all on your Mac, no cloud, no subscription.

🎚️ EBU R128 loudness normalization
🔇 Tiered noise reduction (afftdn / noisereduce)
📝 Whisper transcription + WebVTT captions
🤖 AI shownotes via Ollama (local LLM)
🎙️ Speaker diarization (pyannote.audio)
🖥️ Native macOS .app launcher
