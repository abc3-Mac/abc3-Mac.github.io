Featured Project

CastPolish v1.3.0

A free, local, open-source alternative to Auphonic for podcast audio post-production. Loudness normalization, AI noise reduction, Whisper transcription, AI shownotes, and speaker diarization — all on your machine. No cloud. No subscription.

✅ macOS — full support ✅ Linux — supported ✅ Windows — supported Installer & .app launcher are Mac-only
🎚️ EBU R128 loudness normalization (−16 LUFS)
🔇 AI noise reduction — DeepFilterNet3
📊 Dynamic noise reduction (noisereduce)
📝 Whisper transcription + WebVTT captions
🤖 AI shownotes via Ollama (local LLM)
🎙️ Speaker diarization (pyannote.audio)
📋 Processing log saved with every job
📦 In-app install & update buttons
🖥️ Native macOS .app launcher
⚡ Double-click installer (macOS)
